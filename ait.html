About AIACT&R

An Autonomous Institution under Govt. of NCT of Delhi and affiliated to Indrapratha University, Ambedkar Institute of Advanced Communication Technologies & Research is a seat of higher technical education in India. It was established in year 2001 as Geeta Colony with the objective to meet the growing demands of manpower in the emerging fields of engineering and technology with a close social and industrial interface. Over a period of time the Institute has carved a niche for itself, both nationally and internationally, for excellence in technical education and research.
